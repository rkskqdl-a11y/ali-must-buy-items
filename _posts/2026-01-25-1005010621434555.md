## Technical Specifications & Feature Integration

### 1. Lumination Engine (Touch Bedside Lamp)
The lighting element typically utilizes a high-CRI (Color Rendering Index) LED array. 
*   **Reasoning:** Most units in this price bracket offer stepless dimming via a capacitive touch bar. The logic behind the design is to minimize blue light emission to facilitate melatonin production, making it an ideal nocturnal companion.
*   **Performance:** The 360-degree diffusion ensures even light distribution without harsh hotspots.

### 2. Induction Charging (Wireless Charger)
*   **Protocol:** Qi-standard compatibility.
*   **Power Delivery:** Expected 10W to 15W output.
*   **Safety Logic:** Integrated Over-Voltage Protection (OVP) and Foreign Object Detection (FOD) are essential at this price point to prevent thermal runaway during overnight charging.

### 3. Acoustic Profile (Bluetooth Speaker)
*   **Connectivity:** Likely Bluetooth 5.0 or 5.3 for low-latency and high-fidelity streaming.
*   **Sound Signature:** Given the chassis size, the reasoning suggests a "warm" sound profile optimized for podcasts, acoustic music, and ambient "white noise" rather than bass-heavy EDM.
*   **Portability:** The inclusion of "Portable" suggests an internal lithium-ion battery (typically 1800mAh - 4000mAh), allowing the unit to function away from the DC power source.

### 4. The "6-in-1" Matrix
Beyond the primary three functions, the device generally includes:
*   **Digital Clock/Alarm:** Often with an auto-dimming LED display.
